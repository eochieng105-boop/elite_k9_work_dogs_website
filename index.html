About Our Training

Elite K9 Work Dogs Australia provides professional dog training focused on obedience, behaviour correction, and building strong relationships between dogs and their owners. Trainer Dwight uses positive reinforcement methods and proven K9 training techniques to produce real and lasting results.

Dog Training Services

1-on-1 Dog Training

Personalised training sessions tailored to your dog's behaviour and learning style.

Puppy Training

Teach your puppy essential commands like sit, stay, recall, and proper leash walking.

Behaviour Correction

Stop unwanted behaviours like barking, pulling on the leash, chewing, and aggression.

Dog Socialisation

Help dogs become comfortable around people, environments, and other animals.
